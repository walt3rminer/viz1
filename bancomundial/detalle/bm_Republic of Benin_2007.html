Thus, complementing the activities financed by other sources, the purpose is to help Benin Government implement its new 2006-2010 National Strategic Framework for boosting its national response to HIV/AIDS by contributing to increase and improve the coverage and utilization of prevention services, treatment and care for specific high-risk and vulnerable groups.
