A Parial Risk Guarantee (PRG)to support default risk and transmission risk for the Lake Turkana Wind Project (LTWP)PRG would guarantee $78 million default and transmission risks for this private generation project. With a proposed 300 MW generation, this would be the largest wind energy project in Sub-Saharan Africa. The renewable energy generated by the project will be fed into Kenya's national grid and is expected to make up about 17% of the country's installed power supply in the first year of operation.
