The SPF grant will complement the SIL by: (a) supporting the consolidation of the Hondura's Safer Municipalities Program (SMP) beyond the three project municipalities (subcomponent 1.1) and (b) strengthening local security and criminal justice activities in addition to the SIL's focus on social and situational prevention (subcomponent 1.2) and (c) supporting innovative information, knowlegde and learning activities (subcomponent 1.3).
