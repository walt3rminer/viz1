70000000 Project Development Objective (Note: will be disclosed in the MOS) The PRSC-2's program development objectives (PDOs) focus on strengthening public administration and facilitating private-sector growth. Enhancing governance and improving the business climate are regarded as critical to reducing extreme poverty and promoting shared prosperity. Pillar 1 of the PRSC-2 encompasses good governance, greater financial transparency among public institutions, and more effective PFM. Pillar 2 includes measures to reform the regulatory structure for the private sector and improve the business environment. Pillar 3 focuses on strengthening economic sectors with high growth potential, including governance reforms in the agriculture sector and improved technical and financial efficiency in the energy sector.
