40000000 Project Development Objective (Note: will be disclosed in the MOS) This Development Policy Credit (DPC) is designed to assist the government in managing the downturn, triggered by the collapse of the copper price, by supporting government efforts in the policy areas most affected: (i) the budget, which is very dependent on mining revenues; (ii) social protection, because the poor are badly affected by the downturn; (iii) the financial sector, which was overheating when the global crisis hit, and experienced a major bank failure in late 2008; and (iv) mining, because this sector is the key sector to drive the recovery. The proposed DPC is a single-tranche operation of US$40 million. While it is acknowledged that, because of the magnitude of the shock, the adjustment effort will take place over the next several years, the proposed instrument is a one-off single tranche operation, because of the many uncertainties and risks and the absence of a detailed medium-term policy framework .
