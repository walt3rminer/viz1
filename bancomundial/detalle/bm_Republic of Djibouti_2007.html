2000000 During the early immplementation of the Flood Emergency Rehabilitation Project it appeared that the financial allocations made for the reconstruction of the Ambouli dike -- the main infrastructure to be rehabilitated under the project -- are insufficient. Detailed engineering design and related estimates have confirmed the need of $ 2m additional financing. This was formally requested by the Government, and approved by Bank management. June 22, 2007 This additional financing was submitted to the Board in October 2006 as a credit, as per instructions of the Country Unit. However, given the macro-economic situation of Djibouti, FRM instructed the Region to cancel the credit and re-submit the Additional Financing as a grant. Hence this code is being dropped.
 2000000 The main objective is the rehabilitation and recovery of the economic and social assets damaged by the April 2004 floods. In additio n, the project aims to improve the living conditions in the resettlement zone to which Government has relocated the families whose h ousing was destroyed in the flood-prone area through delivery of basic infrastructure services. Finally, the project plans to build the capacity for long-term disaster prevention and management, and support employment generation for the low-income population, esp ecially from the affected areas through reliance, where feasible, on labor intensive works.
