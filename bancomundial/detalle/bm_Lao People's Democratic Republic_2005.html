 0 This project will conserve globally significant biodiversity in Lao's Bolikhamxay Province. Activities will focus on the Nam Kading National Protected Area (NPA) and two Provincial Protected Areas (PPA) to be identified in the project development phase. The project concept has been endorsed by Laos' GEF Focal Point and is consistent with national priorities of: 1) forest and watershed conservation 2) decentralization, 3) poverty alleviation, and 4) food security. The project will build provincial capacity and initiate activities for ecosystem conservation and contribute to poverty alleviation through sustainable use of wildlife and NTFPs Non-timber Forest Products for villagers through sound wildlife management practices.
