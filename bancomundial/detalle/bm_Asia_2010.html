0 The objectives of this Grant are to (i) improve the international development partners' understanding of the dynamics of selected sub-national conflict areas in South Asia and South-East Asia, with a view to refining and improving the effectiveness of aid approaches in these areas; (ii) provide recommendations for governments to improve the effectiveness of public resources for peace-building in sub-national conflict zones; (iii) strengthen the awareness of policy makers and key civil society stakeholders in relevant countries of relevant experiences and practice in other countries in the region; and (iv) develop a toolkit for analyzing and developing strategies for effective and conflict-sensitive development assistance in sub-national conflict environments, with applicability for Middle Income Countries (MICs).
