0 The overall goal of this project is to help consolidate peace and economic stabi lity and to support sustainable development in the Republic of Congo and in the greater Great Lakes region. The specific objectives of the project are to: (i) Contribute to the improvement of security through disarmament and demobiliza tion of up to 11,000 combatants; (ii) Support social reintegration through rehabilitation of social infrastructur e and conflict management and reconciliation; and (iii) Support economic reintegration through income generating activities to ex- combatants.
