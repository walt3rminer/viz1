10000000 The project aims at supporting the recovery efforts of the Government of Grenada through the financing of critical imports and rehabilitation activities in key social sectors. Financing is provided on an emergency basis to avoid the usual time lag between the end of the relief period and reconstruction. Due to the catastrophic losses in the education and health sectors, and their core importance to Grenadian society, the focus of this project will be to rehabilitate infrastructure in these areas. Specific objectives include: (1),,Supporting the Government's overall reconstruction program through the financing of critical imports; (2),,Restoring school infrastructure to an operational level and ensure a prompt return of children to schools; (3),,Supporting the rehabilitation of the healthcare infrastructure to ensure adequate access to quality health services throughout the island.
