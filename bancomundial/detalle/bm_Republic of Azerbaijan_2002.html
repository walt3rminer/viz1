12920000 The primary objectives of the Project are to : (a) make emergency short term improvements in the water supply system to restore the water supply to Baku, in particular to the poorer elements of the population; (b) improve the water supply system as a whole; and (c) provide the basis for longer term planning and recovery. Government requested IDA assistance through a Supplemental Credit to the ongoing credit to permit financing of the cost of repair and reconstruction of ARWC facilities damaged by the November 25, 2000 earthquake. The Supplemental Credit provides financial assistance for three activities: (i) repair specific components of the Kura and Jeiranbatan water treatment facilities and another operational structure (one warehouse) that suffered damage during the earthquake; (ii) repair sections of the water distribution network and related structures that suffered the most during the earthquake and are causing increased water losses in the system through more frequent burst; (iii) construction supervision and project implementation consulting services.
