Croatia Irrigation Development Project Project Development Objectives The project development objective (PDO) would be to improve water resources planning and management for sustainable use of the water resources in irrigation and drainage through (a) rehabilitation of main infrastructure (b) increased water productivity for irrigated agricultural production; and (c) reform of water resources management institutions and introducing a participatory approach to water management. The primary target beneficiary areas of this project would be the Dubrovnik-Neretva County, Osijek_baranja County and Vukorvar Srijem County. The accomplishment of the above objective would result in a fundamental change in the management of water resources in Croatia and in the economic growth pattern of the agricultural sector there. Preliminary Project Description The PDO would be achieved by successful implementation of the following components: (a) rehabilitation of irrigation and drainage infrastructure: this would include rehabilitation of the main irrigation canals; rehabilitation of pumping stations cleaning and rehabilitation of main drains; (b) introduction of improved irrigated agricultural techniques including pilot irrigated agricultural water savings; demonstrations farms and training; (c) reorganization of Croatia Water (HV) agency, establishing WUAs and water boards, and (d) project management which would include M&E, management information system (MIS), training, office equipment and vehicles.
