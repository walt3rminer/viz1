A key priority is the consolidation of the reforms initiated under the first PRSC series (PRSC-1, 2 and 3) and under PRSC-4 mainly to further refine the regulatory and institutional governance framework which has been developed since 2003, to close existing gaps, and to improve impact of governance related activities. Regarding basic service delivery, the objective is to accompany the process of institutional strengthening and quality improvements underway in the sectors.
