0 The overarching goals of the proposed CDS project is to pilot the use of CDS in Namibia as: (1) an effective instrument to address urban development challenges and; (2) a means of realizing productive, sustainable, inclusive, and well-governed municipalities, towns, and villages in the country. The specific project objectives are: 1. To empower five local authorities to address development challenges in Namibia through CDS; 2. To strengthen the institutional capacity of ALAN so to become an effective service provider to its members; and to enhance its collaboration with the MRLGHRD; 3. To mainstream gender-sensitive and pro-poor civic participation in five local authorities through CDS; and 4. To gather and share CDS knowledge and experience across Namibia and the Region
