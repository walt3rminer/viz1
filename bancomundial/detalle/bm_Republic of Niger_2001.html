 48000000 The objective of the project is to assist the Borrower in increasing sustainable and efficient access to improved potable water supply and sanitation services in urban and rural areas of its territory through: (a) improving water production and distribution efficiency and providing affordable sanitation services; (b) strengthening the institutional capacity of MRE in the areas of regulatory oversight and policy formulation, and strengthening the management capabilities of ARM and SPEN to monitor and regulate water sector services; and (c) rehabilitating rural water facilities, developing on-site sanitation services and implementing community-based management of rural water facilities involving private sector participation.
