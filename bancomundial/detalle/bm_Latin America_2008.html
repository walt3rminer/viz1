 0 To implement the DM2008 winning project. Project's objective is to will link 600 families to a market rewarding the reforestation of reserve land based on the Brazilian #Fomento Florestal# legislation, expanding on a pilot that involves 63 families and has sold over USD 15,000 worth of reforestation credits. Reserve inhabitants will reforest fallow land with native trees and sell credits to local industries that are obliged to offset their wood consumption. The plantings will evolve intodiverse agroforests for the sustainable production of timber and non-timber products.
