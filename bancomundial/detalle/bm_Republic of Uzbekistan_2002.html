40000000 The Development Objective of the project is to improve the safety, quality, reliability, efficiency, financial viability and sustainability of the water supply services in Bukhara and Samarkand. The Development Objective would be achieved through: (a) strategic rehabilitation and efficiency improvement of existing facilities in critical condition; (b) institutional strengthening of the Bukhara and Samarkand Water Utilities (Bukhara City Vodokanal - BVK and Samarkand City Vodokanal - SVK) through a performance-based management contract (SC) with an internationally experienced water utility operator (the Operator); and (c) strengthening of BVK's and SVK's financial capacity through improved financial management and commercial practices.
