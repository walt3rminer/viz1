40000000 The Third Development Policy Operation (DPO-3) is the third in a series of three programmatic development policy operations to support Government's policy reform program to (i) mitigate the impact of the economic downturn in the short-term; and (ii) facilitate recovery and prepare Georgia for post-crisis growth in the medium-term. DPO-3 emphasizes the second objective, to sustain the economic recovery and prepare the conditions for post-crisis growth. In addition to a satisfactory macroeconomic and fiscal framework, themain policy areas supported are: (i) improving the efficiency and effectiveness of public finances; (ii) improving the effectiveness of the social safety net; and (iii) improving external competitiveness.
