180000000 The project's main objectives are to assist the Government in: (a) relieving social and economic hardships(DO1) caused by the April 2002 earthquake in Kermanshah, and the June 2002 earthquake in Qazvin, Zanjan and Hamedan through the reconstruction of damaged public and private assets in the Project Area; and (b) developing an effective monitoring, mitigation and management system (DO2) designed to reduce the impact of future earthquakes.
 20000000 Strengthen the capacity of the Department of Environment in Tehran, Isfahan, Markazi and Khorasan to monitor air quality, and in Khu zestan to monitor water quality.
