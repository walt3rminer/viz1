Project will specifically pilot-test a complete milk cooling system in the hands of 50 smallholders to refine user training instructions and confirm acceptability and profitability before country- or region-wide deployment. It derives from a successfully reengineered beer cooler (originally developed by a German Company) to make it applicable to milk cooling at the smallholder level. The cooler is based on vacuum and zeolite adsorption cooling technology. Along with the previously developed and tested charcoal-powered regenerator the test component will build a complete milk cooling system.
