54000000 The Economic Rehabilitation Credit (ERC) is an integral part of the Bank's Transitional Support Strategy (TSS) to support the transition process and the implementation of the Government's reform program. It would help build the foundation for (i) the improvement of public service delivery and basic social services through promotion of accountability and transparency in the use of public funds, including external assistance, (ii) the Government's efforts to deepen institutional and structural reforms for sustained economic recovery and poverty reduction (iii) the establishment of a track record for early access to relief under the Enhanced Highly Indebted Poor Country (HIPC) Initiative.
