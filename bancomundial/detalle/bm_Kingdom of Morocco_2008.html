This project was under preparation as a GEF "Companion" to the Morocco Integrated Forestry and Rural Development Project (P075561). The main reasons why the Biodiversity Conservation in Bas Draa National Park was dropped were: The Morocco Integrated Forestry and Rural Development Project (P075561) was abandoned because the financing mechanisms conditions required for a successful implementation of the project were not met by the Government. Therefore the idea of a GEF companion was not feasible; The Bas Draa National Park is located on land that the local communities considered as their own collective land and the Bank missions found out that possible conflicts might occur if the project was to be implemented as proposed by the Government; and The preparation process occurred in the middle of a reorganization period within the implementing agency with staff changes and insufficient commitment to the project . The idea of a separate Medium Size Project was considered but not supported by the GEF.
