 120000000 Project Development Objective (Note: will be disclosed in the MOS) The objective of the Pension and Health Finance Reform DPL is to support a comprehensive reform of the pension system in Tanzania. In parallel ,the loan will also support the reform of the health finance system as many of the pension funds also provide health benefits to their participants. The reform will consist of (i) broadening the coverage of the pension system to the entire formal sector through the establishment of a basic, mandatory first-pillar, pay-as-you-go system; (ii) reforming the existing pension pillar from a defined benefit to a defined contribution mandatory second pillar, fully funded system; (iii) transferring the acquired rights of participants in the existing pension system to the first pillar; (iv) opening the possibility of a voluntary third-pillar, fully funded system; and (iv) establishing Bank of Tanzania (BOT) as the single supervisory authority for pension funds. The DPL will fund the fiscal cost of the transition to the new multi-pillar system. Technical assistance for the preparation and implementation of the pension reform will be provided under the existing FSSP. Board Schedule Comments
