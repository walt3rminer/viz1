5000000 The objective of the project is to (i) maintain basic social and economic infrastructure for a significant number of disadvantaged communities and specific vulnerable groups; and (ii) contribute to building the basic capacity of communities and local actors to conduct a local development process which is participatory and transparent. The Project Development Objectives (after restructuring) are as follows: (i) increase access to short-term employment in food-insecure areas (new objective); (ii) increase access to basic social services in poor communities (reformulated objective); and (iii) contribute to building the capacity of communities to plan their development (reformulated objective)
