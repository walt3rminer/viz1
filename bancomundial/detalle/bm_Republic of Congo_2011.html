0 The main objectives of the Republic of Congo EITI program are to: 1) further develop and institutionalize the EITI initiative with special emphasis on engagement by civil society; 2) legislate on EITI principles and objectives to ensure its long term sustainability and strengthen its synergy with other governance reforms; 3) expand the scope and quality of capacity building and outreach programs to improve awareness; 4) conduct audits and investigations of the discrepancies identified by EITI reports and activities, disseminate the results and corrective actions.
