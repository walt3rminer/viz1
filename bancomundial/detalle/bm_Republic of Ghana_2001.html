 220000000 The development objective of the Ghana Road S ector Development Program (RSDP) is to achieve sustainable improvements in the supply and performance of road s and road transport services in a regionally equitable manner. All types of roads are included: trunk roads , feeder roads and urban roads. The road works range from routine maintenance to reconstruction. To achieve better transport services, the program extends beyond road conditions to a variety of road safety measures, improved environmental practices, better reporting and information systems.
