40000000 The development objective of the project is to support the strengthening of the health system in order to effectively combat the major communicable diseases and improve access to quality services for women, children and other vulnerable groups. Within the comprehensive sector development program of the PNDS, the IDA supported project will focus primarily on strengthening sector stewardship, fiduciary systems and monitoring and evaluation (Component1); human resources (Component 2); Infrastructure assessment, mapping and comprehensive rehabilitation plan, including limited construction (Component 3) and, delivery of Package of Essential Services (PSE), with emphasis on Malaria HIV/AIDS and MCH, pharmaceuticals management, community participation/indigenous peoples and equity (Component 4).
