0 A key development objective of the Forestry and Conservation Project (FCP) is to strengthen the capacity of local people, government and non-governmental entities to manage and conserve PNG's forest resources more sustainably. This should result in the long-term maintenance of biodiversity and an increase in the well-being of forest owners. Another key development objective of the project is to improve the quality of life of people living at the local level. Empowering landowners to better conserve, sustainably utilize and benefit from biodiversity on their lands is a national priority for PNG.
