10000000 The Health Investment Fund (HIF) Project aims to improve the health status of the Moldovan population, and to increase the quality and efficiency of the public health sector, by improving access to essential services for the poor. The project also includes support to strategic work, aiming at controlling the TB and HIV/AIDS epidemics. Specific objectives of the project are: (i),,Guarantee universal access to a minimum package of health ,, services; (ii),,Modernize emergency services and primary health care; (iii)Reduce excess capacity in the health sector; (iv),,Strengthen health sector institutional capacity; and (v),,Support the development of TB and AIDS Strategies.
