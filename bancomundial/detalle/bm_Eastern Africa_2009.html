0 The purpose of the collaboration is to: a. Help disaster prone countries, particularly in Sub-Saharan Africa, reduce the risk of disasters, while addressing challenges posed by climate change, and accelerate transition to recovery in the aftermath of a disaster; b. Strengthen disaster risk management capacities of national and sub/regional bodies, parliaments, civil society, and private sector across Africa and beyond; c. Generate and share knowledge and innovation, pooling the existing networks of both institutions, to accelerate implementation of the Hyogo Framework for Action (HFA) in the region.
