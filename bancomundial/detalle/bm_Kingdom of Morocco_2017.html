Improve the financial sustainability of the Caisse Marocaine des RetraitesPillar D. Strengthen oversight of the banking sector.This is the second in a series of two development policy loans (DPLs). The proposed operation supports the long-standing commitment of Moroccan authorities to develop a more inclusive and diversified financial sector. The series supports comprehensive reforms to enable new financing solutions for economic and social infrastructure and small enterprises. In this process, the financial sector takes on more risk, so the program features policies that balance risk-taking with stronger oversight by the regulators. Finally, the reform of the largest pension fund in Morocco ensures that it remains a large local investor in existing and new asset classes. The following institutions in Morocco are involved in the operation: Ministry of Economy and Finance, Bank Al-Maghrib (Central Bank), L’Autorité Marocaine du Marché des Capitaux (capital market authority), and the Autorité de Contrôle des Assurances et de la Prévoyance Sociale (independent insurance and pension supervisor).
