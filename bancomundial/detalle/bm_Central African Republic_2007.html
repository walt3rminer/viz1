18000000 The Project Development Objective (PDO) is to support the Government of Central African Republic (GoCAR) to increase access to infrastructure and urban services in Bangui, the capital city. The project will support GoCAR to rapidly rehabilitate, restore, improve and expand sustainable access to basic infrastructure services for the population of the most deprived districts of Bangui. The achievement of this objective would support GoCAR's efforts to demonstrate visible and tangible improvements in the lives of its citizens that are critical for sustaining social and political stability in the country.
