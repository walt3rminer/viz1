 0 The proposed project has been conceived as a joint effort of the EAR and the IDA to address the current emergency situation in Kosovo, and the general absence of sources of financing to SMEs. The proposed scheme is designed as an interim mechanism to rapidly channel financial resources to private SMEs to re-start production, create employment, and ultimately contribute to poverty alleviation. It also parallels ongoing reforms in the legal and instituional framework in the banking and enterprise sectors that are expected to be supported by subsequent donor/IFI assistance.
