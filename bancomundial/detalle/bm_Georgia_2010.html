 50000000 Project Development Objective (Note: will be disclosed in the MOS) The proposed Second Development Policy Operation (DPO-2) is the second in a series of three programmatic development policy operations intended to support the Government of Georgia's policy reform program to (i) mitigate the impact of the economic downturn in the short-term; and (ii) facilitate recovery and prepare Georgia for post-crisis growth in the medium-term. In addition to a satisfactory macroeconomic and fiscal framework, the main policy areas supported are: (i) improving the efficiency and effectiveness of public finances; (ii) improving the effectiveness of the social safety net; and (iii) improving external competitivness.
