10000000 The development objective of the ERIPTA Credit is to reduce the fiscal deficit and improve the business climate by: (i) downsizing t he parastatal sector; (ii) revitalize the mining sector by strengthening the capacity of the Ministry of Mines and Minerals Developm ent; and (iii) promote private sector business by improving the Companies Act. The project therefore has four components: 1. Investment banking, legal, and other specialist advisory services to support the PRIVATIZATION OF ZCCM; 2. Technical support for the PRIVATIZATION program (for non-ZCCM parastatals) managed by the Zambia Privatization Agency (ZPA); 3. Technical assistance, equipment, and training for the MINISTRY OF MINES AND MINERALS DEVELOPMENT - Completed; 4. Technical assistance to the Legislative Drafting Department of the MINISTRY OF LEGAL AFFAIRS - completed.
 40000000 Address environmental liabilities associated with the mining sector, following the privatization of the mining assets. Mining sector complies with environmental and social regulations.
