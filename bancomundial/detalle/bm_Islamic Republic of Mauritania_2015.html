The objectives of the Project are to ensure increased transparency of payments and revenues from the mining, oil and gas sectors in the Recipient’s territory and to support the Recipient’s compliance with the 2013 EITI Standard.

In concrete terms, the project will finance parts of the Mauritanian national EITI Committee work plan with the objective to generate further in formation on the economic contribution of the extractive sector to the overall economy, increase trust between stakeholders by creat ing space for dialogue and engagement among them, increase knowledge of the decision-making process in the sector, and improve the c ommunication framework.  The project is integrated into the overall EITI workplan 2014-2015 approved by the national EITI Commi ttee.
