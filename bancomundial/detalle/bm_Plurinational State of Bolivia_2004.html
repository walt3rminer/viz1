 30000000 The broader policy objective of this programmatic Loan/Credit is to support the Government's Bank and Corporate Restructuring Program (BCRP), aimed at strengthening the financial and corporate sectors, lowering systemic risks and reducing the probability of crises. This would provide an enabling environment to restart prudent lending, ultimately boosting economic growth. The DOs are grouped into the following areas: Organizational arrangements to manage systemic bank and corporate restructuring; Legal clarity on appropriate distribution of regulatory powers; Bank and corporate diagnosis; Prudential strengthening and prompt corrective action; Bank resolution framework; Corporate restructuring and bankruptcy; Anti-money laundering.
