3500000 The proposed project aims to pilot in locations representative of rural Vanuatu an effective, replicable, and sustainable model fo the provision of basic education years 1-8. Specifically, the project would test whether the Government and other interested stakeholders -- parents, communities, churches and other nongovernmental organizations (especially those engaged in schooling or language research), and international donors -- can work together to introduce successfully preschool and primary education in vernacular languages, improve education quality, and expand educational coverage to junior secondary year 8.
