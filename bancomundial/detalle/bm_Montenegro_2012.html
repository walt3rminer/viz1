79200000 Project Development Objective (Note: will be disclosed in the MOS) The objective of the operation, the second in the series of two programmatic development policy operations, is to support the continued implementation of the financial sector reform agenda started under PFSDPL1. In particular, the proposed PBG will support restoring stability to the Montenegrin banking system system through: (i) implementation of improvements in the regulatory framework; (ii) implementation of supervisory action plans for systemic banks; and (iii) completion of resolution of problem banks.
